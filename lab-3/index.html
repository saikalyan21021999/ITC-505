Delivering Custom Fonts to Browsers

Font Overview: Playfair Display

  1. Font Style: Playfair Display is an elegant serif font known for its sophisticated and classic appearance.
  2. Usage: It is suitable for both headers and body text due to its clear and readable appearance.
  3. Design: Playfair Display features high contrast between thick and thin strokes, providing a refined and stylish look.
  4. Readability: While primarily used for larger text, it remains readable at smaller sizes for short paragraphs.
  5. Compatibility: Widely supported across browsers and platforms, ensuring consistent display.
  6. Performance: As a standard serif font, it offers good loading performance and efficiency.

This section explains how to use a custom font from the Google Fonts CDN.

Steps to Use Google Fonts

  1. Include Google Fonts Link: Add a link to the Google Fonts stylesheet in the `` section of your HTML: 
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Playfair+Display&display=swap">
  2. Apply Font in CSS: Use the font-family specified in the Google Fonts link in your CSS styles: 
    body {
    font-family: 'Playfair Display', serif;
}

By following these steps, the Playfair Display font will be loaded from the Google Fonts CDN and applied to the entire document.
